Selection sort

How it works

Selection Sort finds the smallest element in the list and moves it to the correct position, one step at a time, like selecting the best items from a messy shelf.

  • üîπ Search through the list to find the smallest item ‚Üí Swap it with the first unsorted position ‚Üí Repeat until sorted.

  • üîπ Best & worst case: Always O(n¬≤) since it searches the entire list every time.

  • üîπ Why learn it? It's easy to understand and doesn't need extra memory.

üëÄ Think of it as: Picking the shortest friend in a line and moving them to the front.
