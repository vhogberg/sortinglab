Merge sort

How it works

Merge Sort divides the list into smaller parts, sorts them, and then merges them back together. It's a powerful "divide and conquer" strategy!

  • üîπ How it works: Split into halves ‚Üí Sort each half ‚Üí Merge them back in order.

  • üîπ Best & worst case: Always runs in O(n log n) (much faster than O(n¬≤)!).

  • üîπ Why learn it? It's one of the most efficient sorting algorithms for large datasets.
